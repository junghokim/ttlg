Welcome to Seoulist.

In the fall of 2010 during late night sessions in Sinsa and side alley cafes in Hongdae we hatched a plan to bring the things we appreciated most about Seoul to the English speaking population that had become disconnected from the city we called home.

We're a group of writers, designers, and photographers that have come together under a single banner to do what we love to do most; find a creative outlet to express the essence of what it is that we've come to love about Seoul.
